This weeks Power Herbs

Siberian Ginseng

Powerful herb that improves vitality and stamina. Widely used for chronic fatigue and loss of energy. Helps relieve emotional, mental and physical stress. Additionally helps strenghten the immune system.. What a winner!

Tip: Ginseng is best used in a powder form, organic. You can add this to your smoothies, juices etc. Best not taken before bed as it really does give you more energy!

Mullein

Mullein is on of the greatest herbs used to remove mucus and congestion. Mullein is strongly anti-inflammatory and works great for the endocrine glandular system, especially thyroid. Used for coughs and sore throats. It truly is powerfula herb.

Tip:Mullein is can be used in a tincture (no alcohola added) and also in a form of tea. If you decide to 'drink' it, then always ensure you are getting organic tea.Taste delicious!

Ginkgo Biloba

One of the best herbs for the brain and nervous system. This amazing herb is used throughout the world for memory loss. One of the nature's great tonics. It alkalizes the body strenghtens the heart and vascular system.

Tip:Ginkgo Biloba is best used in a powder form, organic. You can add this to your smoothies, juices etc. You can take it at any time of a day. Your body will love it!
