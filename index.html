This weeks Power Herbs

Siberian Ginseng

Powerful herb that improves vitality and stamina. Widely used for chronic fatigue and loss of energy. Helps relieve emotional and physical stress. Helps strenghten the immune system..
What a winner!

Tip: Ginseng is best used in a powder form, organic. You can add this to your smoothies, juices etc. Best not taken before bed as it really does give you more energy!

Mullein

Mullein is on of the greatest herbs used to remove mucus and congestion. Used for coughs and sore throats, use as soon as you feel unwell..
It truly is a powerful herb.

Tip:Mullein is can be used in a tincture (no alcohola added) and also in a form of tea. If you decide to 'drink' it, then always ensure you are getting organic tea.Taste delicious!

Ginkgo Biloba

One of the best herbs for the brain and nervous system. This amazing herb is used throughout the world for memory loss. One of the nature's great tonics.
Best for your memory!

Tip:Ginkgo Biloba is best used in a powder form, organic. You can add this to your smoothies, juices etc. You can take it at any time of a day. Your body will love it!
